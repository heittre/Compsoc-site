Dr. Aparna Silva, a native of Colombo, completed her undergraduate studies in computer science at the University of Colombo. Passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning, she aspired to pursue a research-oriented career in academia. Her story serves as an inspiration to aspiring researchers in the country and underscores the importance of resilience and resourcefulness in navigating the academic and research career paths in Sri Lanka. CompSoc of University Of Colombo is conducting a panel discussion with Dr. Aparna Silva on the topic of “Academic and Research Career in Sri Lanka”. This will be a very insighful session and we invite all of you to participate in this session!
